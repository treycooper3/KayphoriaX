The Founder

Kayci Chisholm is the founder and CEO of KAYPHORIA X, a luxury couture and lingerie brand founded in October 2019. She earned her BS in Animal Science & Pre-Vet Medicine from Tuskegee University and is pursuing her Master of Professional Studies in Fashion Business from LIM College.

Kayci's journey from runway to brand founder showcases her multifaceted expertise. As a SHEIN Collaborating Designer since June 2022, she creates exclusive capsule collections. Her work as Creative Director at The Model Experience (TME) has positioned her at major fashion events, coordinating runway presentations, editorial shoots, and influencer collaborations at New York Fashion Week and LA Fashion Week.
